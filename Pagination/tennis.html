TENNIS

Tennis is a racket sport that can be played individually against a single opponent (singles) or between two teams of two players each (doubles). The objective is to hit the ball over the net into the opponent's court in such a way that they cannot return it.

Basic Rules:

A match is typically played as the best of three or five sets.
A player wins a set by winning at least six games with a margin of two games.
Points are scored as 15, 30, 40, and game.
