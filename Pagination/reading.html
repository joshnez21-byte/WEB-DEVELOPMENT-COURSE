READING

Reading is one of the simplest and most powerful ways humans expand their inner world. At its core, reading is an encounter—between a mind and an idea, between a voice and a listener, between the present moment and the vastness of imagination or memory. It slows us down just enough to think, yet it can also transport us faster than anything else.
